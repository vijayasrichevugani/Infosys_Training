Permissions
=============
format : service.resource.verb

ex- compute.instances.stop
Role is a collection of permissions

Role can have permissions : appengine.stop
				storage.create
				compute.create

Role -> members- users, google grp, service acnts

Role types:

1- Predefined role

Role : compute

a- Compute.instance.delete
b- compute.instance.list
c- compute.instance.stop
d- compute.instance.get

2- Custom role

Role : myorgneeds

a- compute.instance.start
b- storage.bucket.create
c- compute.instance.setmachine

3- Primitive roles

a- Owner -> All permissions
b- Viewer -> Read only permissions
c- Editor -> Edit permissions
